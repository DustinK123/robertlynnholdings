Who we are

A hands-on owner, not a middleman.

Robert Lynn Holdings owns, develops, and manages commercial and residential property in Fond du Lac, Wisconsin. We aren't a brokerage placing other people's listings — we hold the keys to the spaces we lease and we answer the phone ourselves.

That means tenants get straight answers on terms, faster decisions, and a landlord who has a long-term stake in keeping the property right. Our anchor asset, Harbor View Plaza, sits on Winnebago Drive, with a residential portfolio spread across the city.
