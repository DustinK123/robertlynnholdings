01 / Commercial

Commercial Leasing

We lease retail and office space in the buildings we own — anchored by Harbor View Plaza on Winnebago Drive. Because we're the owner, terms are straightforward and decisions are fast. No listing agent in the middle, no waiting on a third party.

  • Retail and office space leased directly by the owner
  • Clear, business-friendly lease terms and quick decisions
  • Prime corridor frontage in Fond du Lac
  • A landlord invested in keeping the property right long-term
02 / Management

Property Management

We manage what we own, in-house. That means maintenance gets handled promptly, tenants reach a real person, and every space is cared for by people with a long-term stake in the property.

  • Proactive, preventative maintenance programs
  • Responsive tenant relations — you reach the owner, not a call center
  • Careful upkeep and value-add improvements over time
  • Reliable stewardship of both commercial and residential assets
03 / Residential

Residential Rentals

Our residential portfolio spans single-family homes and duplexes across Fond du Lac. Each is owned and maintained locally, so renters get a landlord who's actually reachable and genuinely invested in the home.

  • Single-family homes and duplex units throughout the city
  • Locally owned and maintained — no distant management company
  • Straightforward 12-month leases
  • See current availability on our Properties page
04 / Development

Development

Beyond leasing and management, we take on redevelopment and improvement projects that strengthen the local commercial landscape — upgrading and repositioning property we intend to own and operate for the long haul.

  • Redevelopment and property improvement in the Fond du Lac area

  • A long-term commitment to the local market
